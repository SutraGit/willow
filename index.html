Main Room

Although it may be a little cramped, there is a bed and dinner table, and kitchen. All you need to live. It's also cozy in there!

Another Main Room

Another shot of the interior. With pots, pans, bowls (on the side of the image) and a fire, you can cook food.

Other Things: Church

Everyone who comes here are pilgrims, and every Sunday we go to the church to pray and take ceremory. Join us, it is very peaceful.

Other Things: Church

We also play games and sports, such as draughts (checkers), naughts and crosses (image), and all hid (hide and seek).
